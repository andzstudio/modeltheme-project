How do I write the perfect logo prompt?

The AI understands clear, descriptive instructions. A good prompt acts as a creative brief for the AI engine.

1. Define the Subject & Style

Start with exactly what you want and the artistic style.
Example: "A minimalist geometric logo of a wolf's head, flat vector style."

2. Specify Colors & Background

Tell the AI your brand colors. We recommend asking for a solid background to make background removal easier later.
Example: "Neon blue and cyber pink accents, solid white background."

3. Keep text out of the prompt (Usually)

Most AI image models struggle to spell words perfectly. It's best to generate the icon/symbol first, and add your typography later in a standard design tool, unless using text-specialized models.

How does the AI Logo Generator actually work?

LogoGen utilizes a hybrid architecture, routing your requests to the best specific AI models for different tasks.

OpenRouter API Creation Phase

When you type a prompt, we send it to OpenRouter, which connects to models like Flux or DALL-E. These models are incredible at "imagining" and generating the raw concept art (Text-to-Image).

Recraft AI API Refinement Phase

Once you have your generated image, our Recraft integration handles the professional post-processing. Recraft specializes in structural image changes—like turning a pixel image into a scalable vector (SVG) or cleanly stripping backgrounds.

What is Vectorization (SVG) and why do I need it?

This is the most crucial step for professional logo design. AI models generate raster images (PNGs made of pixels), which get blurry when scaled up. Vectorization fixes this.

Before Vectorizing (PNG/JPG)

Made of tiny squares (pixels). Perfect for screens, but if you stretch it to print on a billboard or a large t-shirt, it will look pixelated and unprofessional.

After Vectorizing (SVG)

Converted into mathematical paths. The logo can be scaled to the size of a building with absolutely zero loss in quality. It is the industry standard for brand assets.

Our system uses Recraft's advanced V4 engine to perfectly trace and vectorize your AI generations.

How do Background Removal and Upscaling work?

Beyond vectorization, we offer two other powerful tools to perfect your assets directly from your Dashboard.

Remove Background

Clicking this button triggers an AI vision model that identifies the core subject of your logo and erases the background. The result is saved as a Transparent PNG, ready to be placed over any color or photo on your website.

Crisp Upscale

If you love a generated image but the resolution is too low, the Crisp Upscale tool uses AI to intelligently add missing pixels and enhance details, effectively multiplying the resolution without blurring.

What is the "Changes Timeline"?

Located in your "My Logos" gallery, the Timeline is a permanent history of every action taken on a specific logo file.

Instead of creating messy duplicate files every time you edit a logo, we keep them organized in a single linear history. You can see exactly when you generated it, and when you applied post-processing.

  • Generated (Original Prompt)
  • Vectorized (SVG created)
  • Background Removed
  • Crisp Upscaled

How are credits deducted per action?

Our platform operates on a transparent credit system. Costs vary depending on the computing power required by the specific AI model you choose.

Dynamic Cost (Generation)

Generating a logo has a variable cost based on the model selected. High-end premium models (like Flux Pro) will consume more credits per image than standard, faster models. The UI will show you the exact cost before generating.

Fixed Cost (Post-Processing)

Subsequent modifications like Vectorization, Background Removal, or Upscaling utilize strict, fixed credit weights per successful action (e.g., Vectorization always costs 1 credit).

How do Annual Plan credit refills work?

Annual subscribers pay only once a year, but our architecture ensures you receive a fresh batch of credits every single month.

A background system continuously monitors your subscription start date. Exactly every 30 days from your billing cycle start date, the system will:

  • Reset your available credits to your plan's maximum monthly allowance.
  • Send you an automated "Credits Refilled" notification email.

Note: Unused credits do not roll over to the next month.

How do I connect my own API keys?

To customize and activate AI features, you can enter your own API keys in the admin panel.

Configuration Steps

  1. Go to LogoGen SaaS → AI API Settings in the WordPress menu.
  2. In the OpenRouter.ai tab, enter your key and save.
  3. Switch to the Recraft.ai tab and enter your Recraft API key.
  4. Click "Test Connection" on each to verify the keys.

All keys are stored securely in the database.
